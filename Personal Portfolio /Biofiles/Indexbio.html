Hi! I'm Elizabeth

Elizabeth Miller is a multifaceted designer, focusing in architectural design, UX/UI design, and visual arts. Born and raised just outside St. Paul/Minneapolis, Minnesota, she made her way over to the East Coast at 18. After completing a Bachelor of Science in Architecture with a minor in Global Studies in Education at the University of Virginia, she moved to Richmond, Virginia. She currently works as an project designer for Architecture Design Office, while freelancing in UX/UI design as well. Along with various architectural design projects across Richmond and surrounding areas, she most notably has directed the branding and oversees selected creative designs for Pocket Properties, a real estate investment startup.
